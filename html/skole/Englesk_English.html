About the subject

Competence target after Vg1 vocational education program



The aim of the training is for the student to be able to

    use suitable strategies in language learning, text creation and communication
    use suitable digital resources and other aids in language learning, text creation and interaction
    use patterns of pronunciation in communication
    listen to, understand and use professional terminology orally and in writing in work situations
    express oneself nuancedly and precisely with flow and coherence, idiomatic expressions and varied sentence structures adapted to purpose, recipient and situation
    account for other people's arguments and use and follow up other people's input in conversations and discussions about occupationally relevant topics
    use knowledge of connections between English and other languages ​​the student knows in their own language learning
    use knowledge of grammar and text structure in work with own oral and written texts
    read, discuss and reflect on content and tools in different types of texts, including self-selected texts
    read and summarize academic content from English-language documentation
    read and compare different non-fiction texts on the same subject from different sources and critically assess how reliable the sources are
    use different sources in a critical, appropriate and traceable manner
    create professionally relevant texts with structure and coherence that describe and document own work adapted to purpose, recipient and situation
    assess and process own texts based on academic criteria and knowledge of language
    describe key features of the emergence of English as a working language
    explore and reflect on diversity and social conditions in the English-speaking world based on historical contexts
    discuss and reflect on form, content and tools in English-language cultural forms of expression from various media, including music, film and games
