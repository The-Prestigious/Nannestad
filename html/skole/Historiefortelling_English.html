About the subject

Competence target after Vg1 vocational education program

The aim of the training is for the student to be able to

produce and communicate content within various genres adapted to visual and auditory media develop and present messages adapted to different target groups and channels explore and use composition principles to ensure good readability adapted to the message and target group choose and use tools, typography and layout that suit different messages and reflect on the effect these have on the message use techniques for idea development, creativity and storytelling in production explore and use storytelling techniques and dramaturgy in own productions exercise source criticism and ethical reflection and apply relevant regulations in own production explore how interactivity in storytelling can be used to create engagement and new expressions, and use this in your own production explore and acquire knowledge about changes in technology and software using various sources explain how the media can influence our opinions, how we perceive others and how others perceive us understand how technology and security can affect open and democratic processes and exercise ethical reflection related to how technology can affect these processes account for and assess how the parties in working life cooperate to develop a better working life
